Our Story

25+ years of vertical transport experience

Sanskar Elevator was established in 2000. Over more than 25 years we have installed, serviced and modernized lifts in homes, hospitals, factories, showrooms and commercial buildings throughout Madhya Pradesh.

The company is led by our Managing Director, Mr. Mahendra Sahu. From our base in Sehore we serve Bhopal, Indore, Ashta, Kurawar, Vidisha, Khandwa and districts across the state. Much of our work comes from customers who have used us before, or who were referred by someone who has.

Our approach is straightforward: recommend the right lift for the building rather than the most expensive one, quote honestly, install it properly, and answer the phone when something goes wrong.

Our Process

How a project runs from start to finish

  1. 1

    Consultation

    We discuss your building, traffic and budget, then recommend a configuration.

  2. 2

    Site Visit

    Our engineer measures the shaft, pit and headroom, and flags any civil work needed.

  3. 3

    Quotation

    You receive a written quotation with the specification and timeline set out clearly.

  4. 4

    Installation

    Our own team erects, wires and commissions the lift to the agreed schedule.

  5. 5

    Testing

    Load, brake, levelling and safety-gear testing before the lift is handed over.

  6. 6

    Maintenance

    Handover into an AMC plan with scheduled visits and 24×7 breakdown cover.
