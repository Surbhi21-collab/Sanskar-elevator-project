About Sanskar Elevator

A trusted elevator company since 2000

Sanskar Elevator was established in 2000 and has spent more than 25 years installing, servicing and modernizing lifts across Madhya Pradesh. Under the leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Mahendra Sahu, the company has grown through repeat customers and referrals.

We work directly with building owners, architects and contractors — from a single home lift in Sehore to passenger and goods elevators for commercial and industrial projects in Bhopal, Indore and across the state.

  • Experienced engineers who handle installation and servicing in-house
  • Quality products and genuine replacement parts
  • Safety-focused installations with ARD fitted as standard
  • Customer-first approach with clear, honest quotations
  • Reliable maintenance support backed by a 24×7 helpline

How We Work

From first call to long-term maintenance

  1. 1

    Consultation

    We discuss your building, traffic and budget, then recommend a configuration.

  2. 2

    Site Visit

    Our engineer measures the shaft, pit and headroom, and flags any civil work needed.

  3. 3

    Quotation

    You receive a written quotation with the specification and timeline set out clearly.

  4. 4

    Installation

    Our own team erects, wires and commissions the lift to the agreed schedule.

  5. 5

    Testing

    Load, brake, levelling and safety-gear testing before the lift is handed over.

  6. 6

    Maintenance

    Handover into an AMC plan with scheduled visits and 24×7 breakdown cover.
